About Unity Youth

Building bridges, fostering unity, and creating opportunities for tertiary students

Who We Are?

ECCA

Unity Youth Organization is a dynamic community of tertiary students committed to academic excellence, social justice, and positive change. Founded on the principles of unity, collaboration, and empowerment, we bring together students from diverse backgrounds and institutions.

Our organization serves as a bridge between different universities and colleges, creating opportunities for cross-institutional collaboration and mutual support. We believe that when students unite with a common purpose, they can achieve extraordinary things.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

Unity Youth Organization was founded by a group of passionate students who saw the need for greater collaboration between tertiary institutions.

2021

First Programs

Launched our mentorship program and organized the first inter-university leadership summit, attracting over 200 participants.

2022

Expansion

Expanded to 25 universities and launched our community outreach initiatives, impacting over 500 community members.

2023

Recognition

Received national recognition for our innovative approach to student collaboration and community engagement.

2024

Growth

Now serving 50 universities with over 500 active members, continuing to grow and make impact across the region.
